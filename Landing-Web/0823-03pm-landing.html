FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How fast is it?

Our target speech‑to‑speech delay is ~1–2 seconds. Per leg latency depends on network quality and device audio paths.

Do both people need the app?

Only one device needs ReMic. The other side can join via QR to a lightweight web session if they prefer.

Which languages are supported?

We’re starting with EN ↔ ZH during early access and will expand based on demand.

Is there a transcript?

Yes. You can view both sides live, save phrases, and end the session with one tap.
